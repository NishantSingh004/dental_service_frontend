FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions (FAQs) about our dental clinic for both patients and staff:

  • For Patients:
    • What should I expect during my first dental visit?

      You can expect a comprehensive examination, including X-rays if necessary, and a discussion about your dental health and treatment options.

    • How can I schedule an appointment?

      Appointments can be scheduled by calling our clinic directly or using our online booking system through our website.

    • Are dental procedures covered by insurance?

      We accept most insurance plans. Our staff can assist you in verifying coverage and understanding your benefits before your appointment.

  • For Staff:
    • What are the clinic's working hours and shift patterns?

      The clinic is open during the following hours:

      • Monday - Friday: 10:00 AM to 05:00 PM
      • Saturday: 10:00 AM to 03:00 PM
      • Sunday: Closed

      Shifts for staff range from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM or 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM, depending on the schedule.

    • How should we handle patient privacy and confidentiality?

      All patient information must be kept confidential. Use secure systems for storing and sharing patient records, and never discuss patient information outside the clinic.
