Privacy Policy

At Healthy Teeth Dental Clinic, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and medical information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details, including name, contact information, and date of birth.
  • Medical history, treatment records, and insurance details.
  • Billing and payment transaction data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide dental care and maintain accurate medical records.
  • For appointment scheduling, reminders, and follow-ups.
  • To process payments and insurance claims.
  • To improve our services and ensure compliance with legal requirements.

3. Data Protection & Security

  • All patient data is securely stored and protected against unauthorized access.
  • We use encryption and security protocols to safeguard electronic records.
  • Only authorized personnel have access to patient information.

4. Sharing of Information

  • We do not share your personal information with third parties without consent.
  • Information may be shared with healthcare providers and insurance companies as required for treatment and billing.
  • We may disclose information if legally required by law or government authorities.

5. Patient Rights

  • You have the right to request access to your medical records.
  • You may request corrections to inaccurate information.
  • You can opt out of receiving non-essential communications.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our privacy practices, please visit our reception or contact us at our clinic.

By using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
