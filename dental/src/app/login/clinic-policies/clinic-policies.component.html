Important Guidelines for Patients & Staff

Welcome to our Healthy Teeth Dental Clinic! To ensure a smooth and safe experience for both our employees and patients, we have established the following policies:

  • Employees and patients must adhere to all health and safety guidelines, including wearing masks and sanitizing hands upon entry.
  • Patients should arrive 10-15 minutes before their appointment for necessary paperwork and check-in.
  • Our clinic follows strict privacy and confidentiality standards to protect patient information.
  • Cancellations should be made at least 24 hours in advance to avoid rescheduling fees.
  • Eating and drinking are not allowed in the treatment areas to maintain hygiene and cleanliness.
  • Parents or guardians must accompany minors (under 18) during their dental visits.
  • Employees must follow proper infection control procedures, including sterilizing equipment after each use.
  • Staff members should maintain professionalism and respectful communication with all patients and colleagues.
  • Mobile phones must be on silent mode in treatment areas to avoid distractions.
  • Patients with medical conditions or allergies should inform the staff before any procedure.

We strive to provide excellent care and maintain a positive and respectful environment. By following these policies, we can ensure the highest quality service for everyone involved.
